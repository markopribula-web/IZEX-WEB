About Us

Civic Association IZEX is a Slovak non-profit organisation promoting innovation, digital education, and community development. Our mission is to enhance digital skills, foster inclusion, and connect education, technology, and society.

Projects

Digital Skills for Youth and Communities (2025–2026)

Workshops and mentoring focused on developing digital competences for students, educators, and communities in Eastern Slovakia.

Local Innovation Network

Connecting municipalities, NGOs, and businesses to promote digital inclusion and innovation in rural areas.

Erasmus+ Cooperation

Building partnerships across Europe to exchange best practices in digital education and training.

Cooperation

IZEX welcomes cooperation with universities, SMEs, NGOs, and public institutions in European projects supporting advanced digital skills, innovation, and community engagement. We are open to partnerships within the Digital Europe, Erasmus+, and Horizon Europe programmes.

Partners

University Partners

Collaboration with European universities and research institutions on projects in digital education and innovation (e.g., TU Košice, Masaryk University, University of Vienna).

Business and Technology

Partnerships with SMEs and technology companies supporting digital transformation, innovation, and training initiatives.

Non-Governmental and Public Sector

Cooperation with municipalities, regional authorities, and NGOs on projects fostering community engagement and digital inclusion.

European Programmes

Active participation in Digital Europe, Erasmus+, and Horizon Europe initiatives focusing on digital skills, education, and innovation capacity building.
